# Amapiano AI - Comprehensive App Overview

## What is Amapiano AI?

Amapiano AI is the world's first artificial intelligence platform specifically designed for creating, analyzing, and exploring amapiano music. It combines cutting-edge AI technology with deep cultural understanding to provide an authentic, comprehensive tool for amapiano music production and education.

## The App in Detail

### Core Functionality

#### 1. AI Music Generation & Transformation
- **Generate from Prompt**: Creates complete amapiano tracks from simple text descriptions. Users can customize BPM, key, mood, and duration.
- **Amapianorize**: A groundbreaking feature that transforms any audio or video file into authentic amapiano style. Users can control the intensity of the transformation and choose between Classic or Private School Amapiano styles.
- **Remix from Source**: After analyzing a track from any source (YouTube, TikTok, local file), users can generate a new track inspired by its musical characteristics.
- **Loop Creation**: Generate specific loops and patterns like log drums, piano, bass, and percussion for use in any Digital Audio Workstation (DAW).

#### 2. Universal Audio Analysis Engine
- **Multi-Source Input**: Analyze audio from YouTube, TikTok, direct URLs, or by uploading local audio/video files from any device.
- **Stem Separation**: The AI separates tracks into individual stems (drums, bass, piano, vocals, other) with high accuracy.
- **Pattern Recognition**: Identifies chord progressions, drum patterns, and basslines, providing insights into the song's structure.
- **Music Theory Analysis**: Detects BPM, key signature, and harmonic structures, offering educational value.

#### 3. Curated Sample Library
- **Authentic Collection**: Thousands of high-quality, royalty-free amapiano samples.
- **Artist Styles**: Samples curated in the style of legendary artists like Kabza De Small and Kelvin Momo.
- **Advanced Search**: Powerful filtering by genre, category, tags, BPM, and key.
- **Diverse Categories**: Includes log drums, piano, percussion, bass, vocals, saxophone, guitar, and synth samples.

#### 4. Interactive Pattern Library
- **Educational Database**: A comprehensive library of chord progressions and drum patterns.
- **Genre-Specific Content**: Patterns for both Classic and Private School Amapiano.
- **Complexity Levels**: Patterns categorized as simple, intermediate, or advanced.
- **Interactive Learning**: Visualize and play patterns to understand their structure, with MIDI export for use in DAWs.
